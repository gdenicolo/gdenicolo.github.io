Lectures

Required textbook for all calculus-based physics courses at SCCC:

ISBN-13: 978-0132275590
Physics for Scientists and Engineers, 4/E   by Doug Giancoli
Publisher: Addison-Wesley
Chapters 1 to 37; without Modern Physics.

Please acquire the complete version, avoid splitting into volumes. This is specially important if you are taking the other courses of this series (PHY230, PHY245 etc) with us. Purchase the complete version.

Essentially, PHY130 covers Chapters 1-13, PHY230 covers Chapters 21-30, and PHY245 covers Chapters 14-20,30-35.

During our classes, we will not use the Mastering Physics CD package that sometimes comes with the textbook. The package can be a great learning tool throughout the semester though: it is up to you.

Acquire this textbook ASAP, and bring it in to all our classes. You can find it listed on Amazon, at our campus bookstore, etc. You are free to search for the best price anywhere !!!
