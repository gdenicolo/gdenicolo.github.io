Lab instructions can be found below:
 

An introductory lab about measurements.

1) One dimensional horizontal motion (x and v versus t)

2) One dimensional vertical motion (ball toss, get g)

3) Two dimensional motion (projectile motion)

4) Newton's second law (force sensor and accelerometer)

5) Coefficient of static and kinetic friction (force sensor)

6) Types of collisions (air tracks)

7) Energy of tossed ball (force sensor and motion detector)

8) Arquimedes and buoyancy

9) Electric circuits

10) The simplest electromagnetic motor
