Lectures

Required textbook for PHY101 & PHY102:

College Physics, 8/E
by Serway, Vuille
Publisher: Brooks/Cole - Cengage Learning

PHY101 covers chapters 1 to 8.
You may choose to purchase only Volume 1 for the PHY101 course.

If you plan to take PHY102 in the future, you will probably save money if you get the combined volume version (i.e., volumes 1 + 2 combined into one single volume).

During our classes, we will not use any CD package that sometimes comes with the textbook. The package can be a great learning tool throughout the semester though: it is up to you.

Acquire the textbook ASAP, and bring it to all our classes. You can find it listed on Amazon, at our campus bookstore, at the publisher's website (www.cengagebrain.com/isbn/978-0-495-55474-5 or www.cengagebrain.com/isbn/0-495-38693-6), etc. You are free to look for better prices anywhere, you do not need to purchase the textbook from the above mentioned sources. Students should also examine the option of renting the textbook: this is a new program available through our campus bookstore and also online. More information can be found at www.rent-a-text.com.
