Hello

A few words about myself

I am a full-time associate professor of physical sciences at Suffolk County Community College (SCCC) in NY. I teach mainly physics courses, and sometimes astronomy too. Before SCCC, I was doing research in astrophysics: that was the topic of my PhD. Nowadays, my main focus is on physics education.
