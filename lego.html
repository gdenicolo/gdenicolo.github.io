Fall 2015 competition

We currently have several LEGO EV3 robotics sets at SCCC that can be used for a fun and instructive competition. All our challenges require students to think well about the design of the robots, and programming of the sensors available in the kit (color, gyro, infrared, ultrasound, touch).
